Become a member

(Condition to be member of OYALD including reference from country way of collecting the funds)

Membership of OYALD is open to all citizens of African countries, and all persons of African descent who:


  1. Are aged between the minimum legal age of maturity in their respective countries of citizenship, and 50 years.
  2. Have functional literacy in English or French.
  3. Are engaged professionally, or as volunteers, in development related activities, or/and who have demonstrated credible support for sustainable development in Africa.

An annual subscribtion of 15 USD is required from each member. This is paid in the Country of registration
