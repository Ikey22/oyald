Why OYALD?

Given the commitments taken by the African Heads of States, to support the implementation of the main development’s Agenda in Africa;

Acknowledging the importance of the Agenda 2063, “The Africa We Want” in building a united and prosperous Africa;

Understanding the strategical contribution of the Agenda 2030, “Living No One Behind” to the amelioration of living condition of people;

Considering the critical role of the youth in changing the narrative of Africa through their meaningful participation to the decisions on the continent;

Convinced that joint work between young Africans, the Civil Society Organization (CSO) and other decision makers in the region can influence policies and enable better life for all;

Having firmly agreed, as young Africans, to contribute to the transformation of our communities, the amelioration of the living condition of people in Africa;

We hereby resolve to establish an association for this purpose, and promulgate for ourselves this constitution to guide the activities of our association.



Who we are?

The 'Organization of Young African Leaders for Development' (OYALD), is headquartered in one country in Africa and established initially in Nairobi, Kenya.

OYALD is a general coordination of all the national chapters, organized and registered in each country of Africa.

Our Vision

A regional youth association established to equip young African leaders for the transformation of the continent.

What we do

INFORM - INSPIRE - INVOLVE

“OYALD, changing the narrative of Africa” The aim of OYALD is to unite young leaders, professionals and entrepreneurs as collaborators towards the achievement of a sustainable development in Africa. Members believe, this is the march to build the new Africa.
